I am a first year PhD researcher at the Delft University of Technology. I completed my undergraduate studies in electrical engineering and master in computer vision. My research interests are optimization and machine learning applied to visual computing. I like designing algorithms for quantitative bio-imaging and analysis. Currently, my focus is processing of 3d point sets captured by single-molecule localization microscopy. These 3d clouds are quite different from the ones obtained by RGBD cameras or Lidar, and challenging to process due to their incomplete and very sparse nature. Our aim is to be the first reaching the 1nm resolution in 3d super-resolution microscopy by registering these structures (:

Check our latest work in Nature Methods where we obtained 5nm resolution from 30% labeled smlm data.
