Hi, I am a first year PhD researcher at the Delft University of Technology. I completed my undergraduate studies in electrical engineering and my master in computer vision & image processing. I have an extensive background in machine learning and optimization. Currently, my focus is processing and registration of 3d point sets captured by single-molecule localization microscopy. These fellas are quite different and challenging due to their under-labeled (generally 70% missing data) and ultra-sparse nature. I aim to reach the 1nm resolution in 3d super-resolution microscopy by fusing these structures (:
